About Us

Flour Bluff Community Outreach Adult Basic Education (ABE) Program provides instruction in reading, writing and mathematics to adults who are seeking to improve the basic skills necessary to function effectively in their personal and family lives, in the workplace, and in the community, and to improve literacy skills necessary for transitioning into the labor market or higher education. Classes are offered in Basic Skills/Pre-GED, GED preparation and English Language Learning for non-native speakers. Instruction is provided through classes or Distance Learning (Internet based).
