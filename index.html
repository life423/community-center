About Us

Flour Bluff Community Outreach Adult Basic Education (ABE) Program provides instruction in reading, writing and mathematics to adults who are seeking to improve the basic skills necessary to function effectively in their personal and family lives, in the workplace, and in the community, and to improve literacy skills necessary for transitioning into the labor market or higher education. Classes are offered in Basic Skills/Pre-GED, GED preparation and English Language Learning for non-native speakers. Instruction is provided through classes or Distance Learning (Internet based).

Our Mission

Flour Bluff Community Outreach Center will be the primary vehicle for educating and providing credentials to those who did not complete high school, need additional skills in the English language or need improvement in literacy skills. High quality services will be offered throughout the community to meet the needs of all segments of the educationally and/or economically disadvantaged youth and adult population. In meeting these community needs, the center will provide customer-driven learning opportunities in a format that promotes the best possible access to services.
